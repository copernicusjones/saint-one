How it works

1. You submit

The agent's claim, what "done" should look like, and your evidence (logs, files, screenshots, repo paths).

2. We verify

We inspect evidence, hash files, run safe validation commands, and perform independent evaluation.

3. You get a verdict

VERIFIED, PARTIALLY_VERIFIED, NOT_VERIFIED, or INDETERMINATE — with a full evidence report.

4. You decide

Use the report to debug, deploy, or go back to your agent with specific feedback.
